Vent It Out: Smoke Intake Filtration System

Did you know that air pollution can cause sickness in our body especially in areas that mixes with smoke air? Well, meet Vent It Out! It's a system that is built for reducing localized air pollution by capturing cigarette smoke before it takes the whole place.

In our project, it uses negative pressure ventilation. An exhaust fan creates a low-pressure zone inside the device. Since air moves from high pressure to low pressure, the surrounding smoke is pulled directly into the system
